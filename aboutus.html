Nuzhat Merchant; the owner of the small home bakery; TLB is a student but has a die hard passion for desserts and baking. Working at home, making cakes and desserts for her loved ones was her hobby, soon the hobby was admired and with the help of her friends' and family’s support, TLB was created.

“I wouldn’t be what I am without them (Her family and friends)”

Not only does she appreciate the support given by her family and friends but also believes that they were the sole reason she took a bigger step in life as she thinks they believed in her more than she believed in herself, and she’ll be forever grateful to them.

TLB avails you freshly made cakes with variety of flavours, be it simple and elegant or fancy and flashy.
